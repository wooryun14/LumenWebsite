How WeGo began

It started with a game. It ended with a family rediscovering itself.

We didn't set out to build a family app. We went for a walk one afternoon — phones out, playing Pokémon Go — and something unexpected happened. Three generations were suddenly moving together, talking about the same thing, exploring the same neighbourhood.

Grandparents were asking questions. Children were the experts. Everyone was outside, looking up, noticing things they'd never noticed before. The game had turned the familiar into an adventure — and the family into a team.

"What if we could design that feeling on purpose — every day?"

🗺️
Discovering your surroundings
The game made us see our neighbourhood differently — parks, landmarks, histories we'd walked past without noticing.
👴
Children teaching grandparents
For once, the youngest were the experts. That reversal opened something tender and new in the family dynamic.
🤝
One goal, every generation
Working together toward the same thing, in the same place, at the same time. That's rarer — and more powerful — than it sounds.

Our story

A healthy family is the foundation of everything. We build tools to help you build it.

Lumen was born from a simple belief — that whole-family health, across every dimension and every generation, is worth taking seriously. Not as a luxury. As a foundation.

We are parents and grandparents who asked what truly matters for children growing up in a complex world. The answer shaped everything we build.

Where Lumen began

Not a business plan. A parenting realisation we couldn't ignore.

We are a family who has lived across cultures and countries. At some point we looked at our children — who held passports from three different nations — and felt the full weight of that reality.

Not anxiety. Clarity. Because the question it forced us to ask turned out to have a beautiful answer.

"What will be the one constant — the foundation — that holds these children wherever they go?"

🌍

Three nationalities. One family.

Three countries shaped who we are. Rather than a complication, we came to see this as the greatest education we could give our children.

🧭

The compass they carry

What stays constant across every border? Strong family. Grounded values. The knowledge of who you are and who you belong to.

Who Lumen is built for

Parents and grandparents are the leaders of their families. Lumen is built for them.

Not managers. Leaders — people who hold the vision, carry the values, and make the quiet decisions that shape who everyone else becomes.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

Parents

The daily architects. Setting routines, modelling values, holding the emotional centre across everything life brings.

👴👵

Grandparents

The long view. Carrying memory, wisdom, and the perspective that no curriculum can replicate.

🌱

Every generation

From a 3-year-old to a grandparent — each holds something the others need. Wellness means keeping that thread unbroken.
